The square is positioned on the day of prescribing. The length of the marker represents how long the dispensing is likely to last for based on the dose instruction. Markers with only the square visible have an unmappable or AS DIRECTION/AS REQUIRED type dose instruction. Hovering over any point of the marker provides additional details.
The yellow filled area is the period for which adherence is calculated for. Any left over medicine (prescribing overlaps) from before this period will also be included.
Hover over dotted line to see x-axis date (useful on larger plots).
Adherence % calculated using total days prescribing (including any carryover from start of follow-up window divided by total days in observation window) and the total number of gap days in the observation window in brackets.
